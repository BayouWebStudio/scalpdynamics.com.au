About

Whether you're dealing with hair loss, thinning, alopecia, or scarring from an injury or hair transplant, SMP offers a non-surgical, low-maintenance solution that restores confidence and transforms how you see yourself. Each session is a precise, artistic process because the devil is in the details.

Based in Manly on Sydney's Northern Beaches, Joey Kintz is a scalp micropigmentation artist with 30 years of professional tattooing experience. He is dedicated to delivering life-changing results. Every procedure is individually crafted from meticulously designed hairlines to perfectly placed impressions that replicate the natural look of hair follicles.

SMP at ScalpDynamics is typically completed over 2–4 sessions with 7-21 days in between to allow the skin to heal completely. This ensures the most natural, long-lasting result possible.
